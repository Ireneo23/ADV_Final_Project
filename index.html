Food Quality

Food quality refers to the attributes and characteristics of food that determine its value and acceptability to consumers. It encompasses factors such as taste, texture, appearance, freshness, and nutritional content, ensuring that food is not only safe to eat but also enjoyable and nutritious. Maintaining high food quality is crucial in meeting consumer expectations, promoting health, and adhering to regulatory standards, making it a key focus in the food industry.
