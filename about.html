About FoodRep

Welcome to FoodRep, a meal planning and recipe management website developed as part of our school project! We're a team of students passionate about healthy eating, technology, and innovative solutions. Through this project, we aim to create an intuitive, user-friendly platform that makes meal planning easy, fun, and accessible for everyone.

Our primary goal with FoodRep is to apply what we've learned about web development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap 5, to build a real-world application that has practical value. This project has given us a unique opportunity to explore key concepts like responsive design, user interface, and data management, which are essential skills in today’s digital world.

Who We Are

We are a group of dedicated students from Holy Cross of Davao College who share a common interest in combining technology and practical life solutions. Each of us brings unique skills to the team, whether it's in coding, design, research, or problem-solving. Together, we’ve worked hard to create FoodRep as a collaborative project that reflects our skills, teamwork, and passion for learning. Our mission is not only to create a useful tool but to grow as developers, thinkers, and team players.

Thank you for visiting and supporting our learning journey. We hope FoodRep helps make meal planning simpler and more enjoyable!
