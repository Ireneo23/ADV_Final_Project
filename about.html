Welcome to FoodRep

Welcome to FoodRep, a meal planning and recipe management website developed as part of our school project! We're a team of students passionate about healthy eating, technology, and innovative solutions. Through this project, we aim to create an intuitive, user-friendly platform that makes meal planning easy, fun, and accessible for everyone.

HI! I'M IRENEO

I am a front-end developer and the leader of our team, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Guerrero Street, Davao City. As the youngest in my family, I was one of the key contributors to the concept of this project, which I dedicate to my late mother. Her work as a chef at a renowned restaurant in Panabo City deeply inspired and influenced me.
